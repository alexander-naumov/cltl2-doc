Chapter 1
Introduction Вступление

Common Lisp is a new dialect of Lisp, a successor to MacLisp [33, 37], inﬂuenced strongly by Zetalisp [55, 34] and to some extent by Scheme [46] and Interlisp [50].

Common Lisp это новый диалект Lisp’а, наследник MacLisp’а [33, 37], под влиянием ZetaLisp [55, 34] и в некоторой мере Schem’ой [46] и Interlisp’ом [50].
